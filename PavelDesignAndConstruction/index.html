Services

We offer a wide variety of services just to make sure that our client are satisfied and all there expectations are met. Some of the key services that we offer are as below:

House Planning

Here we are creating a detailed layout of a residential building, including its rooms, spaces, and dimensions. We are also determining the overall size and shape of the house, as well as the placement and arrangement of its various elements, such as walls, doors, windows, and furniture.

House Renovation and Repair

We improve or restore residential properties to enhance their functionality, aesthetics, and overall value. Some of the house renovations and repairs we do include painting, flooring, roof repair, plumbing upgrades, as well as kitchen and bathroom renovations.

Carpentry

This is the art of working with wood to create various structures and objects. We use a variety of tools to cut, shape, join, and finish wood in order to create furniture, buildings, and other structures. We also specialize in a particular area of carpentry, such as framing, finishing, or cabinetry.

House Construction

House construction is the process of building a residential structure from the ground up. It involves many different steps and requires coordination between various professionals, such as architects, engineers, contractors, and subcontractors.

Interior and Exterior Finishing

Here we make sure work is done to complete the inside of a house, making it habitable and aesthetically pleasing. Exterior finishing refers to the work done to complete the outside of a house, making it weather-resistant, energy-efficient, and attractive and we offer it too.

Plumbing and Water System

This is a network of pipes, fixtures, valves, and other components that deliver and distribute water throughout a building or property, essential for providing clean and safe water for drinking, cooking, bathing, and other daily activities. It consists of both supply and drainage systems.
